Horoscope

As astrologers, we define a horoscope as the astrological forecast of a person or a point in time determined using planetary placements in either the sidereal or tropical Zodiac. The estimates are based on the birth date, and time. That's why a horoscope, like a signature, and is so unique to your month.

You can find your relating sign by using your birth date below.

Aquarius: 21st January - 19th February

Aquarius, you are born in the eleventh house of the Zodiac. You are typically self-sufficient, kind, compassionate, and imaginative. Your fortunate colours are light blue and purple. Your opposing sign is Leo, and you are the least harmonious with Cancer and Virgo. You get along best with Gemini, Libra, and Aquarius.

Pisces: 23rd February - 23rd March

Pisces, you are born under the sign of the twelfth house of the Zodiac. Generally, you are perceptive, trustworthy, caring, and artistic. Purple and  turquoise are lucky colours for you. Virgo is your polar opposite. You are most suitable with Cancer, Scorpio, and Pisces and least suitable with Leo and Libra.

Aries: 21st March - 20th April

Aries, you are born in the first house of the Zodiac. You are generally brave, active, competitive, and forward-thinking. Your best colours are  wine red and cream. Libra is your polar opposite, and you get along the least with Virgo and Scorpio. You get along best with Leo, Sagittarius, and Aries.

Taurus: 20 april - 20 may

Taurus, you are born under the sign of the second house of the Zodiac. Generally, you are loving, loyal, wealthy, and patient. Your fortunate colour is green. Scorpio is your polar opposite, and you get along the least with Libra and Sagittarius. You get along best with Virgo, Capricorn, and Taurus.

Gemini: May 21 - June 21

Gemini, you are born in the third house of the Zodiac. You are generally fast, inquisitive, amusing, and empathetic. Your best colours are light yellow and citrus orange. Your opposing sign is Sagittarius, and you are least suited with Scorpio and Capricorn. Libra, Aquarius, and Gemini are your most harmonious signs.

Cancer: June 22 - July 22

Cancer, you are born under the sign of the fourth house of the Zodiac. You are typically sympathetic, kind, nurturing, and astute.   Your fortunate colours are platinum and cream. Capricorn is your polar opposite, and you get along the least with Sagittarius and Aquarius. Pisces, Scorpio, and Cancer are your most harmonious signs.

Leo: July 23 - August 22

Leo, you are born in the fifth house of the Zodiac. You are typically giving, forceful, loyal, and dependable. Your fortunate colours will be yellow and crimson. Your opposing sign is Aquarius, and you are least suited with Pisces and Capricorn.You get along best with Aries, Sagittarius, and Leo.

Virgo: August 23 - September 22

Virgo, you are born in the sixth house of the Zodiac. You are typically attentive, kind, and considerate. Your best colours are royal blue and grey. Pisces is your polar-opposite. You get along the least with Aries and Aquarius and the most with Taurus, Capricorn, and Virgo.

Libra: September 23 - October 22

Libra, you are born in the seventh house of the Zodiac. You are typically creative, composed, analytical, and endearing. Mid-blues and pale pink are your fortunate colours. Your opposing sign is Aries, and you are least suited with Pisces and Taurus. You get along best with Gemini, Libra, and Aquarius.

Scorpio: October 23 - November 21

Scorpio, you are born under Zodiac's eighth house. You are generally energetic, lively, seductive, and perceptive. Black and crimson are your favorable colours. Taurus is your polar opposite sign. You are least suited with Aries and Gemini. You are most harmonious with Cancer, Pisces, and Scorpio.

Sagittarius: November 22 - December 21

Sagittarius, you are born in the ninth house of the Zodiac. You are typically smart, passionate, forgiving, and upbeat.  deep  Purple and regal blue are your favorable colours. Your reverse sign is Gemini, and you are least suited with Cancer and Taurus. You get along best with Aries, Leo, and Taurus.

Capricorn: December 22 - January 19

Capricorn, you are born in the tenth house of the Zodiac. You are typically cautious, diligent, obedient, and conventional. Saturn is your natal planet. Colours such as black and green usually give you good fortune. Cancer is your polar opposite. You get along the least with Gemini and Leo and the most with Taurus, Virgo, and Capricorn.
